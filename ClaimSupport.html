Packaging Guide

  • Use sturdy boxes to avoid damage.
  • Avoid overloading the box to prevent collapse during shipment.
  • Secure all openings with strong packing tape.
  • Include cushioning materials (bubble wrap, foam) to protect fragile items.
  • Label the package clearly with the destination address and return address.
