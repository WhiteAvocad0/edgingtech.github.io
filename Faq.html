Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?
Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of receipt. The product must be unused and in the original packaging.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping typically takes 5-7 business days for domestic orders and 10-14 business days for international orders.
Do you offer customer support?
Yes, we offer 24/7 customer support via email and phone. You can contact us anytime.
Can I track my order?
Yes, once your order has been shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email.
Do you offer gift wrapping?
Yes, we offer gift wrapping options during the checkout process for an additional fee.
